Chicken Piaccata, Brussel Sprouts, White Rice

FOR THE CHICKEN:
Thinly pounded chicken cutlets
Lemon - 1/3 cup squeezed
Salt, pepper, garlic powder
Fresh garlic- about two cloves minced
Flour
Olive oil
Chicken Broth or Stock- unsalted ½ cup
Capers
1. Preheat skillet to medium-high heat coated with olive oil for frying.
2. Dry off chicken really well and generously season both sides with salt, pepper, and garlic powder
3. Dip each piece in flour for frying
4. Begin frying cutlets, about 3 minutes on each side
5. Remove all chicken from pan, lower the heat a little, and add ½ cup or so of chicken broth to deglaze the pan. Make sure to scrape the bottom of the pan after doing this.
6. Add in minced garlic, capers, lemon juice and put the chicken back in the pan to absorb the sauce for a few more minutes
7. Remove chicken and serve!

FOR THE BRUSSEL SPROUTS:
1 bag of brussels
Olive oil
Salt, pepper
Balsamic glaze
1. Preheat oven to 450
2. Cut up brussel sprouts, cut off ends and make them into smaller chips, and line up on baking sheet.
3. Drizzle with olive oil, salt and pepper
4. Bake for about 30 minutes or until crispy
5. Drizzle with balsamic glaze
(Side- White rice recommendation – trader joes easy jasime white rice- instructions on box)
