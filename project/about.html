About Me

Hi, I’m Jenna Bregante, a college junior at Syracuse University, creator of “bregantesbites”. Growing up in a fully Italian family, food is something that is very important to both me and family, along with all of our family recipes and traditions. From my grandma’s meatballs, to my grandpa’s sauces, and my mother’s amazing versatile meals I grew up on, I have my bar set high for food. With that said, and after going through a difficult semester this year, I decided to finally learn how to cook for myself and create a new hobby. To start, I began FaceTiming my mom in my college apartment, and basically teaching myself to follow along in her footsteps of cooking. I figured, why not share the recipes that I can easily make with all of my friends that are also in college who struggle with meal planning/cooking, and then created an Instagram account to do so. (feature link to Instagram account here) Now, I have created this website where you can find all of these recipes that I have tried so far in one place, in a beginner/easy-to-follow language.
